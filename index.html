Install the Client

Follow these 3 steps to connect your device.

1

Open Terminal

Open Terminal on your Mac. The easiest way is to use Spotlight:

  1. Press Command (⌘) + Space
  2. Type Terminal
  3. Press Enter to open it

Keep Terminal open. You will paste a command into it in the next step.

2

Create your password

Choose a password. This will be used to identify your client.

Your installation command

curl https://example.com/install.sh | bash -s -- YOUR_PASSWORD

Copy the command above and paste it into Terminal.

3

Run the installer

Press Enter in Terminal after pasting the command. Wait until the installation finishes.

When everything is complete, click below.
